Instructions

1. Create profile - supply your jamb number, a password and other personal details as requested.

2. Click 'Create Profile' button, after all the inputs are supplied

3. If profile is successfully created, proceed to login.

4. On successful login, click 'create postutme app' in order to create and submit your application

5. If step 4 above asked for payment, first proceed to pay with e-tranzact, mastercard or Visa and on successful payment, repeat step 4

6. You may edit successfully submitted applications by clicking 'Manage applications' after you have logged in

7. Click on the 'Forgotten Password?'. Reset here on the login form. You will need your security answer and question to give yourself a new password
Click on 'Forgotten Password'. Send to email. You will supply your JAMB number and the email address on record. The system would send your password (on record) to the email address
